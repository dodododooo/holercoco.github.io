Our Essence

Brand Story

Holercoco was born from a simple belief: dogs deserve humane, intelligent solutions. Founded by canine behaviorists and acoustic engineers, we combined ultrasonic precision with smart sensors to create bark control that respects the bond between you and your companion. Every product is a step toward quieter homes and happier dogs.

Vision

We envision a world where every bark is understood — not just silenced. Our vision is to lead the next generation of canine communication tech, blending AI, non-invasive feedback, and seamless design. Holercoco aims to be the trusted partner for modern pet parents seeking harmony without compromise.

Mission

To empower dog owners with effective, safe, and stylish bark management tools. We are committed to continuous innovation, rigorous safety testing, and transparent education. Through technology, we reduce stress — for both ends of the leash — and foster deeper understanding between species.
